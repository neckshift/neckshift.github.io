What is NeckShift?

NeckShift is a free software designed to explore the guitar neck through selections of notes and related scales, all interactively.

It is aimed at both beginner guitarists curious to understand their instrument and scales, and advanced musicians looking for a simple tool to compose or explore more complex structures.

The interface is smooth and works entirely offline. NeckShift uses English musical notation and requires no complex installation — just launch it.

Main Features

Compose from what you play: Select the notes of a riff or chord you just played, and instantly discover compatible scales. No more guessing by ear or searching online — let the tool guide you.

Compatible with any tuning: Enter your tuning, even non-standard ones (Drop C, Open G, etc.), and the tool will adapt the displayed scales automatically. You can even change tuning on the fly.

Interval display: View scales on the neck in note name mode or interval mode, and switch between the two with one click.

Visual scale comparison: Selected scales are shown in blue, hovered scales in lighter shades, so you can instantly see the differences.

Learning mode: Show all scales without playing anything, perfect for quick practice and visualization.
